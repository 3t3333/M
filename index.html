Our Heritage

About Us

At Tic Time, horological mastery meets unparalleled customer service. Nestled in the historic heart of Pasadena, our boutique has been a sanctuary for watch enthusiasts, collectors, and those seeking the perfect timepiece for life's milestones.

We believe a watch is more than an instrument that tells time; it is a legacy passed down through generations. Our curated collection spans from modern marvels of Swiss engineering to rare vintage classics that carry stories of eras past.

With an in-house master watchmaker, we don't just sell timepieces—we preserve them. Whether you are seeking a full mechanical restoration, a simple battery replacement, or your next heirloom, our dedicated team ensures every experience is as precise and enduring as the watches we curate.
